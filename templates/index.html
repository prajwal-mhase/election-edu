Simple Steps
How India's
Election Works
1

Schedule Announced

The Election Commission of India announces the election schedule, triggering the Model Code of Conduct.

2

Nomination Filing

Candidates file nomination papers, which are scrutinized. They may withdraw within a specified period.

3

Campaigning

Parties and candidates campaign to win voter support. Campaigning stops 48 hours before polling.

4

Polling Day

Voters visit their designated booth, verify their ID, and cast their vote on an EVM machine.

5

Vote Counting

EVMs are transported to counting centers and votes are tallied under strict observation.

6

Result Declaration

The candidate with the most votes in each constituency is declared the winner.
